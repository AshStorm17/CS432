About IITGN library

The IIT Gandhinagar Library is a vital resource hub for students and faculty members alike, offering a vast collection of academic literature, research materials, and multimedia resources. It serves as a quiet and conducive space for study, research, and intellectual exploration. With its extensive collection and modern facilities, the library plays a crucial role in supporting the academic and research endeavors of the IITGN community.
